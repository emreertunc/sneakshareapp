About SneakShare

Hey there, welcome to SneakShare; this little yet mighty app. SneakShare is here to help you anonymize and share your URLs in a snap.

What's SneakShare?

SneakShare is a privacy-focused URL cleaner that helps you remove tracking parameters and share clean links.

Features
Clean and secure URLs without tracking parameters
URL shortening capability NEW
View and manage your URL cleaning history
Quick clipboard integration
Share cleaned URLs directly
Open cleaned URLs in your preferred browser
